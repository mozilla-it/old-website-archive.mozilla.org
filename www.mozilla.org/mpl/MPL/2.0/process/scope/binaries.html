Binaries

The MPL’s requirements are stated primarily in terms of source code, but may also be used to cover executables in some circumstances. As a result there may be an opportunity for further clarification of the terms that apply to MPL-licensed executables.

Update: a revised version of Section 3 has been posted for public comment.
