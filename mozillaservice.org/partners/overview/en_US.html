Meet Our Partners

Mozilla Service Week has been made possible with the help of many. Our partners are people who want to make the Web a better place, people who believe in the potential of Mozilla Service Week to transform communities around the world. Without them, we wouldn't be moving forward.

Mozilla Service Week is over for this time, though we hope you will continue to offer volunteer assistance to so many worth while causes. Visit our partners below and see how you can get involved to keep making a difference.
